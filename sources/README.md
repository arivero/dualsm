## Peskin-Schroeder (Textbook)

Peskin and Schroeder, "An Introduction to Quantum Field Theory" (1995), Chapters 16 and 19, is referenced in the contract as `ref-peskin-schroeder`. This is a textbook, not downloadable as a PDF. The relevant beta function and anomaly conventions are:
- T(fund) = 1/2 (matches our convention)
- b_0 = (11/3)C_2(adj) - (4/3)n_f T(fund) where n_f counts Dirac flavours
- Convention verified compatible in `scripts/verify_conventions.py`
