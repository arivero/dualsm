    y_f = y * mu_{0i}^2 / (M_i^2)

    alpha_s(mu) = alpha_s(Lambda_D) / (1 + (b_0 alpha_s(Lambda_D))/(2 pi) ln(mu/Lambda_D))

    and the definition Lambda_QCD = Lambda_D * exp(-2 pi / (b_0 alpha_s(Lambda_D))), derive the relation between Lambda_QCD and Lambda_D.
